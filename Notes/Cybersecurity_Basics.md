# Cybersecurity Basics

## CIA Triad

### Confidentiality
Protects information from unauthorized access.

### Integrity
Ensures data remains accurate and unmodified.

### Availability
Ensures systems and data are accessible when needed.

## Common Cyber Threats

- Phishing
- Malware
- Ransomware
- DDoS Attacks
- SQL Injection
- Brute Force Attacks
